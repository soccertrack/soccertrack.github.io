TrackSpendR

What is TrackSpendR?

A financial tool to track your online spending, sales tax and spending trend across top e-commerce sites.

How does it work?

Click on the store you want to track your spending on. It will spend a few minutes analyzing your spending and generating a spending report on the e-commerce you have chosen.

Privacy

None of your information is stored on our website or data centers. Rest assure, your information is kept with you securely and only you.

Why is this useful?

For small business and individual tax-filer, knowing your sales tax can be additional tax deduction if you are itemizing deduction.
