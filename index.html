What is TrackSpendR?

A financial tool to track your online spending, sales tax and spending trend across top e-commerce sites.

Your browser is currently not supported. Please use Google Chrome browser.

How does it work?

Click on the retailer you want to track. It will take a few seconds to a minute analyzing your spending and generating a spending report on the retailer you have chosen.

Privacy

None of your information is stored on our website or data centers. Rest assure, your information is kept with you securely and only you.

Why is this useful?

For small businesses and individual tax-filers, knowing your sales tax can be additional tax deductions if you are itemizing deduction. Plus understanding your spendings can be helpful with your budgeting.
